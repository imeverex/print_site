The Photographer

Hunting Light
in Wild Places

I shoot in the early dark and the late cold. My work spans glacial rivers and alpine passes, star-saturated skies reflected in still mountain water, and the strange glow of neon against total darkness — the places where civilization ends and something older begins.

Every print is shot, processed, and signed by me. No stock, no AI, no compromises. These are real moments from real terrain.
