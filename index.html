About

Brandon Deurbrouck

I am a photographer drawn to the tension between stillness and motion — the breath held before the shutter clicks.

My work explores the quiet drama embedded in everyday life. Whether in an anonymous urban corner or a remote landscape, I search for frames that feel inevitable in retrospect but were invisible in the moment.

Available for commissions, editorial work, and exhibition inquiries worldwide.
